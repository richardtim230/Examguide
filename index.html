Frequently Asked Questions

Is the ₦150,000 a true lifetime license or are there renewal fees? ▼

Yes, it's a true lifetime license! The ₦150,000 one-time investment grants you unlimited access forever. All maintenance, updates, security patches, and 24/7 customer support are included at no additional cost. You'll never pay renewal fees or hidden charges.

Can I upgrade from Starter to Enterprise plan later? ▼

Absolutely! You can start with the Starter plan and upgrade to Enterprise at any time. If you upgrade later, we'll credit 50% of your Starter plan payment toward the Enterprise plan. No hassle, no long contracts required.

What payment methods do you accept? ▼

We accept multiple payment methods including bank transfers, credit cards (Visa/MasterCard), debit cards, and popular mobile payment platforms. Flexible payment plans are also available for the Enterprise package upon request.

Do you offer implementation and training support? ▼

Absolutely! Our onboarding team provides comprehensive implementation support to get your school up and running smoothly. Enterprise plan customers receive dedicated training sessions, migration support from legacy systems, and a personalized onboarding specialist.

What if I need custom features or integrations? ▼

Enterprise plan customers get API access and can request custom integrations or features. We work closely with your team to understand your unique requirements and develop tailored solutions that fit your institutional needs perfectly.
