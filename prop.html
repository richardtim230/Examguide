Dear Departmental Representative Council,

I hope this letter finds you well. I wanted to share an exciting opportunity, I came across a web development program that teaches students how to build websites and create user interfaces using languages like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It is designed for a wide range of students, from the Faculty of Science to other faculties, so everyone can join in.

I spoke with the coordinator about partnering with our faculty. He explained that the regular registration fee is N5000, which covers two months of training and a certificate stamped by the school, as he plans to work with the ICT team for that. After some discussion, he agreed to a special rate of N3000 for NASSITES, with the condition that the faculty handles the certificates, which can include the faculty name and signature and not compulsorily the academy unless we want to, either in hard copy or digital form.

Since students are currently on holiday, this program could be a wonderful way to keep them engaged and learning from home. It is a productive activity that fits right into their break time.

Key Details

Detail Info
