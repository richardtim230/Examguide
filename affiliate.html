Common Questions

Affiliate FAQ

Find answers to common questions about our affiliate program

Who can become an ExamGuard affiliate?

Anyone can apply! We welcome educators, consultants, technology partners, agencies, and business professionals who can authentically recommend ExamGuard to schools and educational institutions.

When and how do I get paid?

Commissions are calculated monthly based on confirmed referrals. Payments are processed on the 15th of each month via bank transfer, PayPal, or your preferred payment method. Minimum payout is $50.

What is the commission structure?

We offer tiered commissions: 20% for Starter tier (up to 10 referrals), 25% for Professional tier (unlimited), and 30% for Elite tier with dedicated support. Commissions are recurring as long as your referrals remain active customers.

Do you provide marketing materials?

Yes! All affiliates receive access to a comprehensive marketing toolkit including banners, email templates, social media content, case studies, whitepapers, and product guides. Elite affiliates receive custom materials.
